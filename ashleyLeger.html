ASHLEY LEGER

OFFICE MANAGER

EXPERIENCE

I have been working in administrative roles since 2010 and through this experience, had the opportunity to meet and work with a variety of individuals while learning about a multitude of industries. I’m currently responsible for managing the day-to-day operations at the firm and supporting our clients and staff with various administrative needs. My focus is to keep the office running smoothly, so that we can continue to deliver high quality client service.

I graduated with an Honours Bachelor’s degree from McMaster University in 2016 and knew I had a passion for client services. Helping others and knowing my work has made a positive impact has always been rewarding for me. I wanted a career that would allow me to utilize the skills I had acquired throughout my academic and professional life. More importantly, I wanted to continuously be challenged and work in an environment in which no two days would be alike.

My role at the firm allows me to be versatile and contribute to different areas of the business. I work with management on tasks and projects related to marketing, human resources, finance, and administration with the main goal being: how can I make the legal process easier for our clients?
